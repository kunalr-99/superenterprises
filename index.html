Why do you need to opt for these services ?

May it be any kind of climate, we as Mumbaikar's have to face hell lot of challenges in order to protect ourselves from insects in offices as well as at our homes. From these insects, mosquitoes could be the one that can be far more dangerous than we could even imagine. The mosquito's saliva is transferred to the host during the bite, and can cause an itchy rash. In addition, many species can ingest pathogens while biting, and transmit them to future hosts. In this way, mosquitoes are important vectors of parasitic diseases such as Malaria and filariasis, and arboviral diseases such as Yellow fever, Chikungunya, Dengue fever, and Zika.

How do Mosquitoes and other insects harm us?

  • Mosquitoes can act as vectors for many disease-causing viruses and parasites.
  • Viral Diseases such as namely - yellow fever, dengue fever, and chikungunya.

Prevention from diseases caused by insects

  • Elimination of their breeding places.
  • Exclusion via window screens, mosquito nets.
  • Applying insect repellents on one's skin.
  • Biological control with parasites or predators.
